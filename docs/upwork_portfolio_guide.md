# Upwork Portfolio Upload Guide
## Project: Nexoria Commerce Inc. — Finance Automation & Analytics Suite

This guide explains how to upload the **Nexoria Commerce Inc.** project as a high-converting **Portfolio Item** on your Upwork profile. A portfolio item is a gallery piece linked to your profile that showcases your past work, credibility, and expertise to prospective clients.

---

## 📋 Portfolio vs. Project Catalog
* **Portfolio Item (This Guide)**: A free showcase of your past projects. It sits on your profile, acts as proof of your skills, and is linked when you submit custom proposals.
* **Project Catalog (Next Guide)**: A pre-defined service listing with set prices, timelines, and tiers that clients can purchase directly like a product.

---

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Portfolio Upload

### Step 1: Add a New Portfolio Item
1. Go to your Upwork **Profile** page.
2. Scroll down to the **Portfolio** section and click the plus icon (**+**).

### Step 2: Title & Association
* **Project Title**: `Financial Automation & Analytics Suite — Excel VBA, Python & Power BI`
* **Related Upwork Contract**: (Select a past contract if applicable, otherwise leave blank).
* **Completion Date**: Enter a recent date (e.g., today's date).
* **Go to Select Template**.

### Step 3: Choose a Template
* Select the **Case Study** template. 
  > *Why?* This project is structured as an end-to-end business transformation. The Case Study layout allows you to present the *Problem*, *Solution*, and *Results* sequentially, which is highly professional and appeals to executive clients.

### Step 4: Upload Media & Add Details
* **Upload Files**:
  1. **Primary PDF**: Upload `04_budget_vs_actual/Nexoria_BVA_Dashboard.pdf` (This serves as a visual teaser of your Power BI dashboard).
  2. **Technical Diagram**: Upload `docs/architecture_diagram.svg` (To show system integration and database architecture).
  3. **Excel Previews**: Take screenshots of the P&L month dropdown selector (`05_pl_automation`) and the AR aging warning buckets (`06_ar_aging`) and upload them.
* **Project Description**:
  Copy and paste the following text:
  ```markdown
  Nexoria Commerce Inc. is a B2B product distributor operating across 12 US states. Prior to this project, their finance team spent 60% of their time on manual invoicing, state-by-state tax calculation, shipping freight reconciliation, and copy-pasting report templates.
  
  I engineered an integrated financial database layer using SQLite and automated their workflows:
  - Automated monthly billing: Programmatically generated 200+ invoices via a Python ReportLab engine and Excel VBA macro.
  - Multi-state tax compliance: Connected tax rate sheets via Power Query with category exemption rules, saving $8,000/year in billing adjustments.
  - Freight cost allocation: Used Python Pandas to distribute freight bills back to individual orders (70% by weight, 30% by value), improving gross margin precision from 78% to 97%+.
  - Executive visibility: Developed a 2-page star-schema Power BI dashboard in a custom Midnight Navy theme for variance analysis.
  - Operational reporting: Built automated P&L workbooks and Accounts Receivable (AR) aging sheets with green-yellow-red warning alerts using openpyxl and Windows COM.
  - Demand forecasting: Fitted SARIMA models in Python to project 12 months of revenue.
  ```
* **Link to GitHub**: Provide the link to your public GitHub repository where this project is hosted.

### Step 5: Categorize & Tag
* **Category**: Choose **Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A)** or **Data Visualization & Dashboards**.
* **Skills**: Add the following tags to optimize your profile SEO:
  * `Excel VBA`
  * `Power BI`
  * `Financial Analysis`
  * `Data Automation`
  * `Python`
  * `Power Query`
  * `Google Apps Script`
  * `SQLite`

### Step 6: Publish
1. Click **Preview** to review the layout.
2. Click **Publish** to add the project to your profile!
